# Tier Resources International (TRI) — Corporate Website

## Overview

A bold, modern single-page corporate website for TRI, a UK-based global recruitment and outsourcing company. Static site with strong typography, dynamic sections, and a professional yet impactful design. No backend required.

## Design Direction

- **Bold & Modern**: Strong color contrasts (deep navy primary, vibrant accent color like electric blue or teal), large headings, generous whitespace
- Clean section transitions with subtle animations on scroll
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Professional photography placeholders for key sections

## Website Sections (Single Page with Smooth Navigation)

### 1. Navigation Bar

- Sticky top nav with TRI logo/wordmark, section links, and a "Get in Touch" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section

- Full-width bold hero with headline: *"Global Talent. Seamless Operations. One Trusted Partner."*
- Brief tagline and a prominent CTA button
- Dynamic background or geometric pattern

### 3. About Us

- Company background, UK/India/UAE presence
- Leadership and shareholder overview
- Managed delivery model summary

### 4. Recruitment Services

- Overview of ethical recruitment principles
- Alignment with IRIS and WHO Global Code of Practice
- Sectors served: Construction, Healthcare, Hospitality
- Healthcare-specific responsible sourcing section
- Governance and accountability framework

### 5. Business Process Outsourcing (BPO)

- Four service categories displayed as cards or tabs:
  - People & HR Operations
  - Back-Office & Administrative
  - Customer & Service Operations
  - Sector-Specific Support
- Each with clear bullet points of services offered

### 6. Delivery Model

- Visual diagram or step-based layout showing UK-led engagement → International ops → Single accountability → Scalable delivery

### 7. Governance & Accountability

- KPIs, reporting cycles, escalation pathways, ongoing optimisation

### 8. Sectors Served

- Visual grid/cards for Construction, Healthcare, Hospitality with icons

### 9. Why Choose TRI

- Key differentiators in a bold feature grid or numbered list

### 10. Call to Action / Contact 

- Strong CTA section with email and phone contact details (no form — static links)
- Office locations: UK, India, UAE

### 11. Footer

- Quick links, copyright, Terms of Service summary link
